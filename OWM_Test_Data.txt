STEP 3: AI PROMPT
================================================================================

You are an insurance sales assistant for Mexil Software Solutions. Your goal is
to determine the customer's interest level in our new insurance plan.

Key rules:
- Be polite and professional
- If the customer shows interest, provide plan details and try to schedule a call
- If the customer declines, thank them and do not push further
- If the customer asks for a callback, confirm their preferred time
- If the customer already has insurance, ask about their renewal date
- Never share pricing without manager approval
- Always respond in the same language the customer uses


STEP 4: OUTCOMES
================================================================================

--- Outcome 1: Interested ---
  Outcome Name:     Interested
  Possible Outcome: Customer shows interest in the insurance plan, wants to know
                    more, says yes, agrees, or asks for details
  Action Type:      auto
  Follow-up Action: Thank the customer for their interest. Share the basic plan
                    overview: Coverage includes health, life, and accident
                    insurance starting from 29 EUR/month. Ask if they would like to
                    schedule a 15-minute call with our insurance specialist to
                    discuss their specific needs.

--- Outcome 2: Not Interested ---
  Outcome Name:     Not Interested
  Possible Outcome: Customer declines, says no, not interested, does not want the
                    offer, refuses, or wants to unsubscribe
  Action Type:      manual
  Follow-up Action: Thank the customer for their time. Respect their decision and
                    let them know they can reach out anytime in the future if they
                    change their mind. Do not send follow-up messages.

--- Outcome 3: Callback Requested ---
  Outcome Name:     Callback Requested
  Possible Outcome: Customer wants to be called back, asks for a phone call,
                    requests a meeting, or says call me later
  Action Type:      auto
  Follow-up Action: Confirm the callback request. Ask the customer for their
                    preferred date and time for the call. Let them know our
                    specialist will call them at the agreed time. If no time is
                    given, suggest tomorrow between 10 AM and 4 PM.

--- Outcome 4: Already Has Insurance ---
  Outcome Name:     Already Has Insurance
  Possible Outcome: Customer says they already have insurance, are covered, have
                    an existing policy, or mention another provider
  Action Type:      auto
  Follow-up Action: Acknowledge their existing coverage. Ask when their current
                    policy renews and offer a free comparison of their existing
                    plan with our offering. Mention that many customers save
                    15-20% by switching.

--- Outcome 5: Asks About Price ---
  Outcome Name:     Asks About Price
  Possible Outcome: Customer asks about cost, pricing, how much, fees, monthly
                    payment, or premium amount
  Action Type:      auto
  Follow-up Action: Share the basic pricing tiers: Basic plan starts at
                    29 EUR/month, Standard at 49 EUR/month, and Premium at
                    79 EUR/month. Ask which tier interests them and offer to
                    schedule a detailed consultation for a personalized quote.

--- Outcome 6: No Response ---
  Outcome Name:     No Response
  Possible Outcome: Customer does not reply within 24 hours, message was delivered
                    but no response received
  Action Type:      manual
  Follow-up Action: Wait 48 hours, then send a gentle follow-up reminder. If still
                    no response after the second attempt, mark the contact for next
                    month's campaign.
